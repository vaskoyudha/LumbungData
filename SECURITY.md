# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of LumbungData seriously. If you believe you've found a security vulnerability, please report it to us by emailing security@lumbungdata.org.

**Please do NOT publicly disclose the issue until we've had a chance to address it.**

### Our Process

1. **Acknowledgement**: You'll receive an acknowledgement within 48 hours of your report.
2. **Investigation**: We'll investigate the issue and may contact you for further details.
3. **Resolution**: We aim to provide a fix for validated vulnerabilities within 90 days.
4. **Disclosure**: Once fixed, we'll coordinate a public disclosure with you.

### Scope

This security policy covers:
- Data integrity vulnerabilities.
- Offline sync and P2P communication flaws.
- Blockchain contract bugs in the subsidy ledger.
- Unauthorized access or data leaks within the PWA.

### Rewards

LumbungData is a community-driven open source project. While we cannot offer monetary rewards at this time, we'll gladly acknowledge your contribution in our release notes and Security Hall of Fame (coming soon).
